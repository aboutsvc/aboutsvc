THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT, HOW WE USE IT, AND THE LIMITED WAYS IN WHICH WE SHARE IT

Information is automatically collected when you use our App. Information collected may include usage details, metadata, and real-time information about the location of your device. We do not generally collect or store information by which we ourselves may personally identify you ("Personally Identifiable Information" or "PII") through the App. Any data is held by service providers and co-operation partners only, which are outlined below. Please note, however, that the information we have about you might still qualify as "personal data" under the GDPR.

Analytics Data

Anonymous data, which we refer to as “Analytics Data,” regarding your use of our App is tracked and transmitted to us by our Apps themselves. Analytics Data consists of:

  • A device identifier enabling other issues on the same device to be located;
  • Crash logs, enabling application failures to be investigated; and
  • App interaction signals and event data, typically indicating when an application feature has been used ina certain way.
We use Analytics Data to fix and improve the App and evaluate features, including if and when users use different features, and how quickly new features are found. Analytics Data is anonymized, meaning it cannot be traced back to you. We process Analytics Data using Google Analytics, Firebase, and Microsoft App Center, but do not otherwise share it.

Advertising Data

Like many apps, our App is in part supported by advertising. Unless you opt-out, discussed more below, we may serve ads to you by transmitting to our select advertising partners a standard advertising identifier (an “Advertiser ID”) that we collect from your device. Advertising IDs are standard across the mobile advertising industry and are used to identify a particular device for advertising purposes. Advertising IDs are random identifiers that are generated by and tied to your mobile device. An example from a Google Android device looks like this: cdda802e-fb9c-46af-9846-0233d394c712.

Advertising IDs are typically generated once per device, so your device would always be associated with the same Advertising ID. Advertising IDs don't contain any Personal Information in and of themselves. However, because they are typically static, a device will maintain the same Advertising ID over time, it is possible for third parties to use an Advertising ID in conjunction with location data they could collect from your device or from other apps you use, and other apps known to be associated with your device's Advertising ID, to create inferences about you. Both Apple's iOS or Google's Android operating system provide a mechanism for users to reset or disable their device's Advertising ID. If you elect to disable your Advertising ID, we respect that choice and will not send your device's Advertising ID to our advertising partners.

Our applications transmit a device's Advertising ID without adding any other Personal Information or other additional information. We believe this results in an excellent user experience with relevant and helpful advertising, while also protecting your privacy and safety.

Purchase Data

When you purchase a subscription or other in-app item from the Apple App Store or Google Play store, that transaction is processed by the relevant app store. We have no access to your payment instruments and can see only the very limited view of the transaction shared with us by the app store. The limited data we receive consists of an order ID generated by the app store, price information, the date and time of the purchase, and may in some cases contain your ZIP code of the purchaser. This limited purchase data is only used by our support staff to request refunds for you when appropriate, and is not shared with any third party.

CHOICES ABOUT YOUR INFORMATION

We strive to provide you with choices regarding the information you provide to us. This section describes mechanisms we provide for you to control certain uses and disclosures of your information.

  • Advertising IDs. You may object to the transfer of your Advertising ID at any given time with immediate, future effect by adjusting the settings on your mobile device to, depending on your operating system, disable the Apple-Ad-Tracking via IFDA or to opt out of interest-based ads via the Google advertising ID. The mechanism for opting of out of interest-based ads is different for each device, so please consult the user manual for your device's operating system for specific instructions. Additionally or alternatively, you can reset your Advertising ID at any time (and as frequently as you would like), which causes Google's or Apple's system to build your device's profile for advertising purposes anew, so that advertisers cannot link your activities taking place prior to the reset to your device.

Please note that we do not access your device's do-not-track settings, and we therefore do not respond to such settings. Third party advertisers, ad networks, and ad servers that display ads in our App may, however, access and respond to your device's and browsers' do-not-track signals; if you would like to know how a third party advertiser responds to do-not-track signals sent by your device or browser, please consult their privacy policy.
  • Targeted Advertising by Third Parties. We do not control third parties' collection or use of your information to serve interest-based advertising. However, these third parties may provide you with ways to choose not to have your information collected or used in this way.
